Who We Are

My name is Kagiso Lencoe, and I founded KTD (Keep The Drip) in 2023. KTD is more than just a clothing brand, it’s a lifestyle. I started this business to give young people stylish, affordable, and authentic streetwear that makes them feel confident in their own skin.

Our Mission

At KTD, our mission is simple: to inspire and empower the youth through streetwear that represents culture, confidence, and unity. Every hoodie and T-shirt we design is created with the idea of keeping the drip alive.

Our Vision

My vision is to grow KTD into South Africa’s top youth streetwear brand and expand across Africa. I want KTD to be more than a clothing line, I want it to be a movement that represents style, hustle, and authenticity.
